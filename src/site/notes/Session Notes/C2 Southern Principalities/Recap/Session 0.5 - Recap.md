# Session 0.5 - Recap

> [!quote]
> "Every shadow has a purpose. Tonight, yours passed unnoticed."

## Summary

Under the cover of another ordinary evening in **In'nali**, a Lotus Choir cell quietly carried out its first operation together.

While each operative performed a different role across the city—from observation and political cover to infiltration and escape—the objective remained simple: recover a designated archive scroll without drawing attention.

The mission unfolded almost exactly as planned.

With the scroll secured, the team regrouped near the Great Lift and departed the capital beneath the guise of another routine government convoy.

No alarms were raised.

No pursuit followed.

To anyone watching...

Nothing unusual had happened.

---

## Major Events

- Successfully recovered the requested archive scroll.
- Each member completed their assigned role without compromising the operation.
- Escaped In'nali aboard a political transport without incident.
- The team begins another assignment under the banner of the Lotus Choir.

## Important Discoveries

Nothing about the recovered scroll has been revealed to the team.

For now, it is simply another successful operation completed in service to the Choir.
