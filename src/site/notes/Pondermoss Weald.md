# Pondermoss Weald

> "Every root remembers."

## Overview

The Pondermoss Weald is an immense ancient forest occupying much of the northeastern Southern Principalities.

Unlike many forests, the Weald is not defined by danger or wilderness alone. It is a place of memory.

The oldest trees predate kingdoms, dynasties, and recorded history. Beneath their roots lie forgotten roads, abandoned shrines, ancient tombs, and the remains of people whose names have long since vanished from the world.

Many travel to the Weald seeking answers.

Many leave with more questions.

---

## Geography

The Weald consists of rolling wooded hills, deep glades, moss-covered ridges, and immense groves of ancient zelkova.

Notable features include:

- colossal goldenheart zelkova trees
- mist-covered valleys
- hidden monasteries
- forgotten burial grounds
- ancient pilgrimage routes
- spirit-haunted clearings

The forest is vast enough that entire settlements can exist beneath its canopy without ever seeing another village.

---

## The Goldenhearts

The most iconic trees of the Weald are the goldenheart zelkovas.

These immense trees can grow hundreds of feet tall and are revered throughout the South.

Their roots often consume ruins, shrines, and tombs, preserving them beneath living wood.

Many believe the trees possess a memory of their own.

---

## House of the Forgotten

Hidden deep within the Weald stands the [[House of the Forgotten\|House of the Forgotten]].

The monastery serves as caretaker of those who have been abandoned by history.

The monks maintain countless records, graves, and memorials dedicated to individuals who would otherwise be lost to time.

---

## The Silent Tombs

Throughout the forest lie thousands of burial sites.

Some belong to heroes.

Others belong to tyrants.

Most belong to people whose stories have already faded.

Many locals believe disturbing these places invites misfortune.

---

## Culture

People of the Weald value:

- wisdom
- remembrance
- patience
- ancestry
- preservation

The region attracts historians, monks, scholars, and pilgrims from across the Southern Principalities.

---

## Magatsuchi

The Great Kaiju [[Compendium/Lore/Deities/Kaiju/Magatsuchi\|Magatsuchi]] is most often associated with the Pondermoss Weald.

Its appearance is viewed as an omen that hidden truths are surfacing.

Stories differ wildly regarding the creature's nature, though nearly all agree that its arrival precedes significant change.

---

## Economy

The Weald exports:

- rare hardwoods
- medicinal herbs
- historical records
- paper
- inks
- crafted woodworks

Its greatest value, however, is knowledge.
