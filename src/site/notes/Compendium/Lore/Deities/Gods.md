># The Gods of Dawn (The Anunnaki)
> *" When the Gods rose, the world grew ordered. When the Titans bled, the world grew wild. Yet still, the blood and the law contest the land."*  
> —High Hymn of Salren

---

## Overview
The **Gods of Dawn**, known in the oldest texts as the **Anunnaki**, are the pantheon who warred against the [[Compendium/Lore/Deities/Titans\|Titans]] in the **[[Compendium/Lore/Events/Ash War\|War of Ash and Dawn]]**.  
Unlike the primal, living essence of the Titans, the Gods embody **hierarchy, decree, and inevitability**. They did not *grow from the land* but *descended into it*, bringing order through edicts and celestial law.  

Where the Titans are remembered as vast and chaotic, the Gods are remembered as structured and stern — **law over chaos, destiny over choice, civilization over wilderness**.  

---

## The Divine Hierarchy
The Anunnaki are less a family than a **council of dominion**, each presiding over an aspect of order. Mortals often rename them, but their oldest titles endure in sacred texts.

---

### **Enlil → Auramar, Lord of the Skies**
- **Domain:** Storms, kingship, command.  
- **Symbol:** A crown wrapped in lightning.  
- **Role:** Supreme among the Dawn Gods; his decree cast the Titans into silence.  
- **Legacy:** Priests of Auramar enforce obedience, declaring that his storm is the law of heaven itself.  

---

### **Enki → Thalyon, Keeper of the Deep**
- **Domain:** Knowledge, craft, rivers, hidden wisdom.  
- **Symbol:** A flowing vessel pouring unending water.  
- **Role:** Architect of civilization, teacher of law and craft.  
- **Legacy:** Worshipped by scholars, but whispered to be sympathetic to mortals who stray from divine order.  

---

### **Ninhursag → Asvara, the Mother of Foundations**
- **Domain:** Fertility, earth, birth, the hearth.  
- **Symbol:** A mountain split by a tree.  
- **Role:** Giver of structure to mortal life — bloodlines, homes, boundaries.  
- **Legacy:** Her temples are tied to law of inheritance and blood-right.  

---

### **Inanna → Seraphis, Lady of Dominion**
- **Domain:** War, desire, rulership, ambition.  
- **Symbol:** A spear entwined with roses.  
- **Role:** Enforcer of Auramar’s rule on the battlefield and in court.  
- **Legacy:** Worshipped by nobles and generals; feared as a goddess of both glory and ruin.  

---

### **Utu → Kalem, the All-Seeing**
- **Domain:** Justice, truth, the sun.  
- **Symbol:** A blazing disk flanked by scales.  
- **Role:** Judge of mortals and gods alike, seer of hidden truths.  
- **Legacy:** Judges in [[Compendium/Atlas/Material Plane/Rushthan/Droshar/Imperial Heartlands/The Capital/Salren/Salren\|Salren]] swear oaths by Kalem’s light. His worship is stern and exacting.  

---

### **Ereshkigal → Veymora, the Silent Queen**
- **Domain:** Death, inevitability, shadow.  
- **Symbol:** A black gate beneath a waning moon.  
- **Role:** Keeper of the underworld, unbending in law of death.  
- **Legacy:** In [[Compendium/Atlas/Material Plane/Rushthan/Droshar/Droshar\|Droshar]], her cult is quiet but ever-present — death always claims her due.  

---

### **Nanna → Selurion, the Moon-Father**
- **Domain:** Time, cycles, omen, guidance.  
- **Symbol:** A silver crescent over still water.  
- **Role:** Bringer of calendars, keeper of fate’s rhythm.  
- **Legacy:** Sailors and farmers both look to Selurion for when to act and when to wait.  

---

## The Tablets of Destiny
Central to the Anunnaki’s myth is the **[[Tablets of Destiny\|Tablets of Destiny]]** — divine artifacts said to contain the decrees of reality itself.  
- **Function:** Whoever holds the Tablets determines the laws of existence.  
- **Role in the War:** Used to bind the Titans, chaining their essence beneath the earth.  
- **Modern Rumors:** Some claim fragments of the Tablets lie buried in [[Compendium/Lore/Objects/Leylines\|leyline]] nodes, their edicts cracked, causing instability.  

---

## Relationship with the Titans
- **Enemies by Nature:** The [[Compendium/Lore/Deities/Titans\|Titans]] embodied living, mutable chaos; the Anunnaki sought to fix the world into law.  
- **[[Compendium/Lore/Events/Ash War\|The War of Ash and Dawn]]:** Their conflict reshaped [[Compendium/Atlas/Material Plane/Rushthan/Droshar/Droshar\|Droshar]] — the Titans bled into leylines, the Gods etched order into the heavens.  
- **The Balance:** Druids whisper that neither side won — the world is the battlefield still.  

---

## Worship in Droshar
- **Imperial Faith:** The Empire of [[Compendium/Atlas/Material Plane/Rushthan/Droshar/Imperial Heartlands/The Capital/Salren/Salren\|Salren]] venerates the Dawn Gods as their patrons, with Auramar and Kalem central to rulership.  
- **Frontier Distrust:** In [[Compendium/Atlas/Material Plane/Rushthan/Droshar/Northern Reaches/Emberreach/Emberreach\|Emberreach]] and the [[Compendium/Atlas/Material Plane/Rushthan/Droshar/Northern Reaches/Northspires/Northspires\|Northspires]], the Dawn Gods are seen as distant tyrants, while druids hold to the titans’ legacy.  
- **Hidden Sects:** Some cults secretly seek the Tablets of Destiny, hoping to rewrite the order of the world.
