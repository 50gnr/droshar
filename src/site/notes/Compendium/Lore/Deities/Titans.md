# Titans


> [!quote|no-t]
> *"They are the roots of mountains, the breath of storms, the fire in the world’s heart. They do not die, they only change."*  
> —Ravyn Wolfsdaughter
> 
> > “Before the gods learned to speak the world into order, the Titans had already sung it into being.”  
> — _Fragment of the [[Leyline Codex\|Leyline Codex]], Verdant Coil Archives_
---

## Overview
The **Titans** were the primordial architects of [[Compendium/Atlas/Material Plane/Rushthan/Droshar/Droshar\|Droshar]] — colossal beings of living stone, flame, frost, and sea. They were neither divine nor mortal but something older, born of the world itself.  
When the **[[Compendium/Lore/Events/Ash War\|War of Ash and Dawn]]** broke out between Titans and Gods, the Titans fell — buried, shattered, or stilled. Yet their essence lingers. **[[Compendium/Lore/Objects/Leylines\|Leylines]]** are said to be their lifeblood, and **[[Compendium/Lore/Objects/Titan Bones\|Titan Bones]]** the remnants of their bodies.  

Where their remains lie, the land itself bends, mutates, and breathes with strange power.

---

## The Known Titans

### **Thyrris, the Stone Father**
- **Domain:** Mountains, stone, and endurance.  
- **Form:** A vast, hunched figure whose ribs became the mountain ranges of the [[Compendium/Atlas/Material Plane/Rushthan/Droshar/Northern Reaches/Northspires/Northspires\|Northspires]].  
- **Fate:** Buried beneath the peaks, his bones still anchor ley fractures.  
- **Legacy:** Miners whisper of “stone veins” that pulse like blood; druids say avalanches are his restless stirring.  

---

### **Moruun, the Breath of Storms**
- **Domain:** Wind, thunder, and chaos of the sky.  
- **Form:** A serpentine titan of cloud and lightning, said to coil across entire horizons.  
- **Fate:** Struck down by the Dawn Gods, her spine is now the jagged storm coast west of [[Khorbael\|Khorbael]].  
- **Legacy:** Leyline storms crackle with her voice; sailors swear her laughter is in every hurricane.  

---

### **Draugrith, the Frost-Blooded**
- **Domain:** Ice, silence, and winter’s grip.  
- **Form:** A towering giant clad in endless rime, whose breath froze whole valleys.  
- **Fate:** Shattered in the War, fragments of her form became glaciers of the [[Compendium/Atlas/Material Plane/Rushthan/Droshar/Northern Reaches/Northspires/Northspires\|Northspires]].  
- **Legacy:** The frost giants claim descent from Draugrith’s marrow, calling her the *Mother of Silence*.  

---

### **Ignovar, the Ember-Heart**
- **Domain:** Fire, magma, and creation through destruction.  
- **Form:** A burning colossus, heart blazing with molten core.  
- **Fate:** His fall carved the southern volcanic ridges near [[Compendium/Atlas/Material Plane/Rushthan/Droshar/Southern Principalities/Bardune/Bardune\|Bardune]].  
- **Legacy:** Black glass plains, ember-veined mountains, and flame elementals trace their origin to his corpse.  

---

### **Nerithys, the Tideshaper**
- **Domain:** Seas, currents, and depths unknown.  
- **Form:** A titan with arms of coral and hair of waves, said to stride across oceans.  
- **Fate:** Drowned in the War; her body became the trench west of [[Khorbael\|Khorbael]].  
- **Legacy:** Sea giants call her the *First Mother*, while krakens hate her for her intrusion.  

---

### **Veythar, the Verdant Coil**
- **Domain:** Growth, beasts, and tangled wilderness.  
- **Form:** A serpent of root and vine, whose coils became forests and jungles.  
- **Fate:** Poisoned during the War, his corpse rotted into swamps and overgrown ruins.  
- **Legacy:** Biomancy may be his lingering will — a reminder that life adapts, even if twisted.  

---

## Theories on Survival
- **The Dreaming:** Druids insist the titans are not dead, only dreaming. Ley surges are their restless turning.  
- **The Sealed:** Some cults claim the gods bound the titans in chains of light, buried deep until mortals are “ready.”  
- **The Awakening:** Recent instability in [[Compendium/Lore/Objects/Leylines\|Leylines]] flows has many fearing that the Titans may rise again, rekindling the **[[Compendium/Lore/Events/Ash War\|War of Ash and Dawn]]**.
