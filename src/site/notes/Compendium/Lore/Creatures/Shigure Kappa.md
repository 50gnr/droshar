## Overview

Nobody remembers exactly when the Kappa first wandered into the compound.

At some point...

Everyone simply accepted that he lived there.

He spends most of his time around the natural hot springs, streams, and koi ponds, occasionally emerging to investigate visitors, steal unattended belongings, or demand cucumbers from the kitchens.

Despite his antics, the Shigure family has never seriously considered asking him to leave.

---

## Description

About three feet tall, the Kappa has moss-green skin, a turtle-like shell, webbed hands, and a shallow bowl atop his head that is perpetually filled with crystal-clear spring water.

His oversized eyes constantly dart from person to person, searching for unattended towels, sandals, snacks, or anything else that might prove entertaining.

He moves surprisingly quietly for someone who rarely stops giggling.

---

## Public Reputation

Within the Shigure Compound:

- harmless nuisance
- towel thief
- bathhouse mascot
- cucumber enthusiast

Visitors quickly learn one important lesson:

Never leave your belongings unattended near the springs.

---

## Personality

The Kappa is endlessly curious.

He enjoys observing newcomers almost as much as confusing them.

Although fond of harmless pranks, he has never intentionally injured anyone within the compound.

Most residents secretly enjoy his company...

Though few will admit it after chasing him across the gardens.

---

## Favorite Mischief

- Steals towels.
- Rearranges sandals.
- Hides wooden practice swords.
- Swaps tea cups.
- Watches sparring matches from the water.
- Startles sleepy bathers with loud splashes.
- Accepts cucumbers as peace offerings.

---

## Relationship to the Compound

Everyone pretends to be annoyed.

Everyone would notice if he disappeared.

Even Ayame quietly leaves cucumber slices near the springs from time to time.

She insists they're "for the garden."

Nobody believes her.

# Hidden Data

## Role in the Campaign

The Kappa exists to remind the players that not every yokai is dangerous.

He brings warmth, humor, and a little unpredictability to the Shigure Compound.

He should appear just often enough that players start asking where he is whenever they return home.

---

## DM Notes

### Core Theme

A harmless spirit that has quietly become part of the family.

### Presentation Notes

- Curious before cautious.
- Never speaks Common.
- Expressive body language.
- Tilts his head frequently.
- Always watching.
- Vanishes into water almost instantly.

### Important

The Kappa is never the joke.

His antics are.

Treat him like a beloved pet that simply happens to be an ancient yokai.
