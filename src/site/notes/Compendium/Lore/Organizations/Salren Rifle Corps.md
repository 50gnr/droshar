# Salren Rifle Corps
>![rifle corps.png\|right wm-sm](/img/user/Assets/Images/Lore/Magitech/rifle%20corps.png)> “Measured force. Anchored power. One shot, one decree.”
> The **Salren Rifle Corps** are the Empire’s most disciplined magitech infantry.  
They are not line soldiers. They are **precision deployments**.
>
>The **[[Compendium/Atlas/Material Plane/Rushthan/Droshar/Imperial Heartlands/The Capital/Salren/Salren\|Salren]] Rifle Corps** are the Empire’s most disciplined [[Compendium/Rules & Homebrew/Magitech\|Magitech]] infantry.  
They are not line soldiers. They are **precision deployments**.
>
>Where conventional troops hold territory, the Rifle Corps enforce policy.

---

## Overview

- Estimated Strength: ~100–150 total active personnel  
- Standard Unit: 12 riflemen + 1 Lieutenant  
- Deployment: Envoy escort, political enforcement, special operations  
- Equipment: Magitech twin-barrel action rifles (Frail–Robust grade cores)  

They are rarely fielded in mass.  
When they appear, it is deliberate.

---

## Doctrine

The Rifle Corps operate under three principles:

1. **Precision over volume**
2. **Control over spectacle**
3. **Containment over conquest**

They are not shock troops.
They are not skirmishers.

They are the Empire’s statement that:
> “Power has been calculated.”

---

## Uniform & Appearance

Lean, fitted, disciplined.

- Long reinforced field coats (dark slate or iron-blue)
- Minimal plate — no bulky armor
- Reinforced leather bracers for recoil stabilization
- High-collared underlayers with resonance damp stitching
- Polished boots, travel-ready

Helmets:
- Sleek, crested
- Subtle essence sigil at the brow
- More officer than conscript

They resemble engineered soldiers — not trench infantry.

---

## Emblem

The Corps’ insignia features:

- A crowned vertical rifle silhouette
- Wings flared outward (symbol of range, not flight)
- A faceted essence crystal at the heart
- Encircled by the motto:

> **“Anchored Through Fire.”**

The crystal is always depicted stable — never cracked.

---

## Standard Weapon: Twin-Gaze Pattern Rifle

A two-barrel, break-action magitech rifle.

- Single-shot per barrel
- Manually reloaded
- No magazine feed
- No rapid-fire mechanism

Each rifle contains:

- A chambered Essence core (usually Frail or Robust grade)
- Arcanomagnetic rotational ring assembly
- Stabilized rifled barrels (stacked vertically)

The design enforces discipline.
Every shot is intentional.

---

## Essence Grades in Corps Use

- **Frail** — standard issue, common patrol use  
- **Robust** — officer grade, stable under extended firing  
- **Potent+** — restricted to high command or experimental deployment  

Mythic and Deific grade cores are never standard issue.

---

## Role Within Salren

The Rifle Corps serve primarily as:

- Escort for Processional Engines
- Protection for Imperial envoys
- Enforcers of frontier compliance
- Strike teams against rogue biomancers

They are the Empire’s visible proof that:

Magitech is not theory.  
It is authority.

---

## Cultural Reputation

Frontier view:
> “If the long coats arrive, someone important has already decided.”

Biomancer view:
> “They cage the Weave and call it discipline.”

Imperial view:
> “Order, in twelve rifles.”

---

## Notes

The Rifle Corps are rare by design.  
Salren limits their numbers intentionally.

To mass-produce them would cheapen what they represent.

They are not the Empire’s army.

They are its calibration.
