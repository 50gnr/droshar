## Overview

Rei Kurogane is one of two apprentices currently training within the [[Compendium/Atlas/Material Plane/Rushthan/Droshar/Southern Principalities/Shigure Compound\|Shigure Compound]].

Created as a Haniwa guardian and raised under the guidance of the Shigure family, Rei approaches every lesson with unwavering discipline and patience. While still years from earning a place among the Lotus Choir's field operatives, few doubt that day will eventually come.

Where others rely upon speed or instinct...

Rei relies upon consistency.

---

## Description

Rei resembles a young warrior sculpted from weathered terracotta. Fine seams mark where his body was originally formed, while tiny cracks and softened edges speak to years of relentless training rather than neglect.

He dresses simply in an indigo training gi embroidered with the Shigure crest and is almost never seen without a polished wooden bokken resting at his side.

Despite his clay body, his movements are remarkably fluid.

Every motion appears deliberate.

Every stance balanced.

---

## Public Reputation

Within the Shigure Compound:

- dependable
- disciplined
- hardworking
- quiet
- impossible to distract

Among Lotus Choir operatives:

> "If Rei says he'll do it... consider it done."

Though young, he has already earned a reputation for never abandoning a task before it is finished.

---

## Personality

Rei speaks little.

Not because he is shy.

Because he prefers observation over conversation.

He believes improvement comes through repetition, discipline, and honest effort.

Praise makes him uncomfortable.

Failure motivates him.

---

## Philosophy

> "Master the first step before chasing the hundredth."

Rei believes there are no shortcuts worth taking.

Every technique begins with the fundamentals.

Every accomplishment is built one patient day at a time.

---

## Relationship to [[Compendium/NPC's/Kaede Mizuno\|Kaede Mizuno]]

Training partner.

Closest friend.

Constant rival.

The two spar nearly every morning, each convinced the other still has much to learn.

Their competition has become something of a daily spectacle around the compound.

---

## Relationship to [[Compendium/NPC's/Southern Principalities/Ayame Shigure\|Ayame Shigure]]

Rei has enormous respect for Ayame.

He arrives early for every meal, completes every assigned chore without complaint, and quietly helps wherever he notices work needing to be done.

---

## Relationship to [[Compendium/NPC's/Southern Principalities/Daichi Shigure\|Daichi Shigure]]

Though Daichi rarely comments on his progress, Rei values every lesson the aging operative offers.

A single nod of approval from Daichi is worth more to him than a dozen compliments from anyone else.

---

## Relationship to the Party

Rei regards the party with quiet admiration.

They are everything he hopes to become someday.

Though reluctant to ask questions directly, he pays close attention to how each member approaches problems, solves conflicts, and carries themselves beyond the battlefield.
