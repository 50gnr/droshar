## Overview 
A magistrate in [[Compendium/Atlas/Material Plane/Rushthan/Droshar/Southern Principalities/Bardune/Bardune\|Bardune]] whose position was once preserved through a Lotus Choir operation involving [[Compendium/Party/Player Characters/Nobumasa Shigure\|Nobu]]. 
The operation succeeded. The cleanup did as well. 
During that cleanup, Nobu uncovered information that could place the magistrate in a vulnerable position if ever revealed. Because of this, the magistrate remains useful. --- 

## Relationship to Nobu 
The magistrate owes Nobu. 
Nobu maintains the relationship because it has value, not because he mistakes obligation for loyalty. 
As long as their interests align, the magistrate may provide assistance, favors, documents, or introductions within Bardune. ---

# Hidden Data

## Presentation Notes 
- Polished and careful. 
- Speaks like every word might someday be recorded. 
- Helpful when cornered. - Never openly hostile. 
- Always measuring risk.  

## Truth 
The magistrate is not loyal to Nobu. They are afraid of what Nobu knows. That fear can be useful. It can also become dangerous if the magistrate believes silence is safer than cooperation. 

## Story Use 
- Bardune contact 
- Political favor 
- Legal protection 
- Access to records 
- Future betrayal if pressure grows
