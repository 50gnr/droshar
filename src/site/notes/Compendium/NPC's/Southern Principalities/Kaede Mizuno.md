## Overview

Kaede Mizuno is one of the two apprentices currently training within the [[Compendium/Atlas/Material Plane/Rushthan/Droshar/Southern Principalities/Shigure Compound\|Shigure Compound]].

Bright, quick-witted, and endlessly curious, Kaede has a habit of discovering solutions nobody else considered. She has an uncanny knack for reading people, finding hidden paths, and asking questions that leave even veteran operatives thinking.

Where Rei perfects the expected...

Kaede explores the unexpected.

---

## Description

Kaede is a young Tanuki whose rounded ears, striped tail, and expressive face make hiding her emotions nearly impossible.

She wears practical travel clothes beneath an oversized indigo training haori that always seems one size too large. Her sleeves are filled with bits of string, folded paper, chalk, lock picks, and other objects she insists might become useful later.

Nobody knows where half of it comes from.

Ayame stopped asking years ago.

---

## Public Reputation

Within the Shigure Compound:

- clever
- mischievous
- resourceful
- endlessly curious
- usually one step ahead

Among Lotus Choir operatives:

> "If something's missing... ask Kaede."

"...and if she says she didn't take it, she's probably telling the truth."

Probably.

---

## Personality

Kaede meets every new problem with curiosity instead of caution.

She enjoys puzzles.

She asks questions others overlook.

She talks while thinking.

She rarely stays still for long.

Although she enjoys teasing Rei, she is fiercely loyal to the people she considers family.

---

## Philosophy

> "There's always another way."

Kaede believes flexibility wins where strength cannot.

Plans should change.

People should adapt.

And if something doesn't work...

Try something unexpected.

---

## Relationship to [[Compendium/NPC's/Southern Principalities/Rei Kurogane\|Rei Kurogane]]

Training partner.

Closest friend.

Favorite person to annoy.

Every spar ends with Kaede insisting Rei is too predictable.

Every rematch ends with Rei quietly proving her wrong.

Neither keeps score.

Both absolutely know the score.

---

## Relationship to [[Compendium/NPC's/Southern Principalities/Ayame Shigure\|Ayame Shigure]]

Kaede spends almost as much time helping Ayame as she does creating extra work for her.

She has an unfortunate talent for finding trouble.

Fortunately...

She also has an equally impressive talent for fixing it.

Usually.

---

## Relationship to [[Compendium/NPC's/Southern Principalities/Daichi Shigure\|Daichi Shigure]]

Daichi rarely answers Kaede's endless questions.

Instead...

He responds with questions of his own.

She secretly enjoys those conversations more than anyone realizes.

---

## Relationship to the Party

Kaede immediately latches onto visiting operatives.

Every returning mission means:

- new stories
- new techniques
- strange souvenirs
- impossible questions

She is fascinated by the wider world beyond the compound walls.
