## Overview

Renji Maki is the older brother of [[Compendium/Party/Player Characters/Player Facing/Maki\|Maki Maki]].

For many years, most believed Ren had simply disappeared.

Whether by choice or circumstance, no reliable record of his whereabouts has surfaced since.

Only recently have rumors begun placing him back within the Southern Principalities.

---

## Description

Like his younger brother, Ren possesses a sharp mind and an observant nature. Age has done little to soften him. He carries himself with the confidence of someone accustomed to making difficult decisions and living with their consequences.

Though they share many physical features, where Maki's presence is warm and inviting, Ren's is measured and quietly unsettling.

He studies people before speaking.

When he finally does...

Every word feels chosen.

---

## Public Reputation

Very little is known.

Among those who claim to have met him recently:

- travels frequently
- asks unusual questions
- pays well for information
- avoids unnecessary attention
- disappears before authorities arrive

Many dismiss the stories as rumor.

Others quietly stop talking when his name is mentioned.

---

## Personality

Ren is intelligent, patient, and remarkably difficult to read.

He prefers understanding a situation before acting and rarely wastes effort on emotional outbursts.

Unlike his brother, Ren has little interest in winning people over.

He would rather understand what motivates them.

---

## Philosophy

> "Truth has a habit of surviving."

Ren believes that every carefully constructed story eventually develops cracks.

His work is simply to find them.

---

## Relationship to [[Compendium/Party/Player Characters/Maki Maki\|Maki Maki]]

Ren and Maki have not seen one another for many years.

Whatever once existed between them has long since been replaced by unanswered questions.

Whether reconciliation remains possible...

Neither brother truly knows.

---

# Hidden Data

## Role in the Campaign

Ren serves as:

- recurring personal antagonist
- downtime complication
- source of uncomfortable truths
- reminder that every decision leaves consequences

He should appear infrequently.

Each appearance should matter.

---

## The Truth

As children, Ren repeatedly attempted to ruin Maki's future through lies, manipulation, and false accusations.

The Lotus Choir investigated the claims.

Finding Maki innocent, they quietly removed Ren from his brother's life.

Ren survived.

Whether he escaped, was exiled, or simply allowed to disappear remains unknown.

For years he has lived believing the Lotus Choir stole the life that should have been his.

From Ren's perspective:

- He exposed corruption.
- The Choir silenced him.
- His younger brother accepted the opportunity.
- Then became a Minister.

He does not believe Maki earned his position.

He believes it was handed to him.

---

## Goals

Ren is not interested in revenge through violence.

He wants something far more satisfying.

He wants Maki's reputation to collapse under the weight of the truth—or what people believe to be the truth.

If Maki loses the public's trust...

Ren wins.

---

## Story Use

Ren works best through:

- rumors
- anonymous letters
- missing documents
- witnesses from the past
- political embarrassment
- inconvenient truths
- unexpected encounters during downtime

He should rarely attack directly.

His preferred battlefield is public perception.

---

## DM Notes

### Core Theme

The past that refuses to stay buried.

---

### Presentation Notes

- Calm.
- Never hurried.
- Speaks softly.
- Holds eye contact.
- Smiles very little.
- Never raises his voice.
- Always seems one step ahead.

### Important

Ren is **not evil**.

He is a man who has spent years convincing himself that he was the wronged brother.

Whether he is right...

Whether he is wrong...

Whether both brothers remember the past differently...

Those answers should unfold slowly over the course of the campaign.
