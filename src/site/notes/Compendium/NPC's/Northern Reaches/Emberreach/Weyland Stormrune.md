># Weyland Stormrune
> *"Steel and scars, we’re cut from the same stone."*  
> —Tovrik Ironhand

---

## Overview
Weyland Stormrune is the **Guildmaster of the [[Compendium/Atlas/Material Plane/Rushthan/Droshar/Northern Reaches/Emberreach/Ashen Lodge/Ashen Lodge\|Ashen Lodge]]** in [[Compendium/Atlas/Material Plane/Rushthan/Droshar/Northern Reaches/Emberreach/Emberreach\|Emberreach]], a man defined by discipline, endurance, and silence. Broad-shouldered and silver-haired, he carries himself with the weight of battles fought long ago, though he speaks little of them. His presence commands respect not through charm or titles, but through the sheer gravity of his gaze.  

He is a mentor, taskmaster, and sentinel all at once — shaping Emberreach’s hunters not into heroes, but into survivors.  

---

## Appearance
- Weathered features, scarred by claw and blade.  
- Silver hair falls loosely across his face, often tied back when training.  
- Wears a battered cloak marked with old beast-scars, never repaired — a silent record of victories.  
- His eyes hold no cruelty, but a constant measure, as though weighing the worth of everything he sees.  

---

## Personality
- **Stoic:** Rarely shows humor or warmth, but his nod of approval carries more weight than a feast-day’s praise.  
- **Demanding:** Believes hunters must be forged through hardship. Those who break were never meant to stand in the North.  
- **Observant:** Says little, but notices everything. His silence in council often speaks louder than words.  
- **Unyielding:** Holds Emberreach’s independence above all — though his reasons remain his own.  

---

## Relationships
- **Eira Mossroot:** Respects her skill but mistrusts her hunger for dangerous knowledge.  
- **Tovrik Ironhand:** A kindred spirit, though their stubbornness often clashes.  
- **Ravyn Wolfsdaughter:** Sees her mysticism as impractical, but knows she is rarely wrong.  
- **The Hunters (PCs):** Watches closely, testing constantly. His rare praise is a prize in itself.  

---

## Rumors
- Some say Weyland once slew a monster too great for any hunter to name, and that its bones lie hidden beneath the Lodge.  
- Others whisper he has not aged naturally — that the leylines themselves keep him alive, scar by scar.  
- The boldest claim he has faced death a dozen times and returned each, carrying a secret even Emberreach does not suspect.  

---

## At the Table
When Weylund speaks, keep it short, firm, and final. He is a man who wastes no words — his silence, and the weight behind his eyes, do the talking.
