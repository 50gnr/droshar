## 🎭 Introduction (5-Sentence Method)

A tall golden-scaled dragonborn stands slightly hunched over a stack of field notes, pince-nez sliding down the bridge of her snout.  
Her blue eyes move constantly, cataloguing details faster than conversation can keep up.  
She scribbles observations mid-sentence, barely looking at the parchment as her pen scratches across the page.  
Travel stains mark her coat pockets, each bulging with specimen jars, folded sketches, and bone fragments.  
“Fascinating… absolutely fascinating. I need but one moment.”

## 🧬 Profession

Anthrozoologist and naturalist specializing in **rare ecosystems and anomalous fauna**.

Her research focuses on:

- species adaptation
- environmental mutation
- predator hierarchies
- unexplained ecological anomalies

She is currently studying the wildlife and biome interactions surrounding **[[Compendium/Atlas/Material Plane/Rushthan/Droshar/Imperial Heartlands/The Tethynglas/The Tethynglas\|The Tethynglas]]**.

---

## 🎯 Current Work

Dr. Doolots established a **small research site along the river basin south of [[Compendium/Atlas/Material Plane/Rushthan/Droshar/Northern Reaches/Emberreach/Emberreach\|Emberreach]]**.

Her goal is to catalogue previously undocumented species within the forest.

However, several field assistants have gone missing.

Local trappers attribute the disappearances to a **“swamp curse.”**

Doolots suspects a **large apex predator or ecological anomaly**.

She previously contracted mercenaries to investigate.

They claimed success.

The disappearances continued.
