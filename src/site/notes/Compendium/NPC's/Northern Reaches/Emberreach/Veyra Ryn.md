>A retired dragonborn huntress who once faced a Ancient Dragon and lived, Veyra moves with quiet grace despite her limp. She is a legend, though she speaks little of her past.

Average height covered in scales of white with piercing blue eyes, her tale scared and callused as any good weapon should be! Although long time retired for a handful of various reasons, she still dawns a sturdy breastplate over her tunic which is marked with a sigil of a snowy peak above the clouds. Now manages the daily affairs of the Ashen lodge while [[Compendium/NPC's/Northern Reaches/Emberreach/Weyland Stormrune\|Weyland]] keeps track of the hunters, politics, training and contracts.

Veyra took it upon herself to raise a a baby outcast that was found by a hunting party that was roaming the [[Compendium/Atlas/Material Plane/Rushthan/Droshar/Imperial Heartlands/The Tethynglas/The Tethynglas\|Tethynglas]] on a hunt.  After days of watching a restrained creature the [[Compendium/Atlas/Material Plane/Rushthan/Droshar/Northern Reaches/Emberreach/Ashen Lodge/Ashen Lodge\|Ashen Lodge]] council decided he is not a threat but a sentient being, an abomination albeit but a sentient one. This creature spent years within the lodge guided by Veyra's firm hand and stern direction, he was taught how to clean, cook, hunt, fish, and survive the wilderness, The last part came naturally i must admit!
