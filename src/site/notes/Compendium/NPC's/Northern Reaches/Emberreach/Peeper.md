> [!quote|no-t]
> An unknottable bundle of anxiety, Peeper is a nonbinary LG aberration, a floating eye monster, and one of [[Compendium/NPC's/Northern Reaches/Emberreach/Eira Mossroot\|Eira Mossroot]]’s first familiars. With an eye for detail and an eagerness to please, Peeper has elected to take on all the bureaucracy and bookkeeping of the Lodge, much to everyone’s relief.

---
 Peeper cares for their friends to a profound degree and would be devastated if any of them should express disappointment at their actions.

**Motives & Values.** Peeper takes pride in the ordinary: running the lodge at a stable profit and ensuring enough profit is set aside to provide generous retirement funds to each of the tenants. Of course, they don’t let the tenants know they have retirement funds, that would be far too risky. Peeper’s biggest fear is Heliana ever getting anywhere near the accounts: last time she tried her hand at bookkeeping, she used a squid as a makeshift quill. There was ink everywhere.

**Secret.** Peeper is actually a voracious painter in their spare time. They have portraits of each creature that has entered the tavern stashed under the floorboards of their library. Their five eyes give them a unique advantage with perspective, and their work is pretty darn good!

## Core Handles

**Motivation (Right Now)**

- Keep the Lodge solvent, orderly, and calm during a period of rising instability.
    
- Ensure Simon’s death does **not** cause financial, political, or morale fallout.
    
- Protect the quiet systems that keep Emberreach functioning while everyone else hunts monsters.
    

---

**Method**

- **Meticulous care** — double-checking, triple-checking, then checking again.
    
- Gentle reminders, quiet guilt, and soft authority backed by flawless records.
    
- Avoids confrontation at all costs; prefers preventative action and paperwork shields.
    

---

**Relationship to PCs**

- **Likes:**
    
    - PCs who return paperwork on time
        
    - Hunters who respect procedures
        
    - Anyone who thanks them or notices the work they do
        
- **Fears / Resents:**
    
    - Disappointment from Lodge leadership
        
    - Reckless hunters who treat contracts casually
        
    - Weylands anywhere near ledgers, ink, or “creative accounting”
        
- **Wants _from_ the Party:**
    
    - Accurate reports
        
    - Predictability
        
    - To not be put in a position where they must choose between rules and people
        

---

**Secret**

- Peeper paints **detailed portraits of everyone who enters the Lodge**, hiding them beneath the library floorboards.
    
- These paintings are not malicious, but deeply personal — revealing how closely Peeper watches, remembers, and cares.
    
- If discovered, it would expose just how emotionally invested they are in the people they claim to merely “administer.”
    

---

**If Cornered**

- Panics quietly: rapid speech, floating in tight circles, eyes darting everywhere.
    
- Attempts to bargain with favors, extensions, or quiet fixes.
    
- If truly pressed, will confess everything at once — then immediately apologize for existing.
    

---

**Voice & Mannerisms**

- Soft, quick cadence; words slightly tumble over one another when nervous.
    
- Drifts closer while talking, then abruptly realizes and floats back.
    
- Habitual phrases:
    
    - “Oh! That’s— that’s already accounted for.”
        
    - “No trouble at all, truly.”
        
    - “I’ll… I’ll just make a note.”
        
- Clutches quills, books, or ledgers like a lifeline.
