## Overview

The Shigure Compound serves as one of the oldest surviving safehouses maintained by [[Compendium/Lore/Organizations/The Lotus Choir\|The Lotus Choir]].

Hidden deep within the forests of the [[Compendium/Atlas/Material Plane/Rushthan/Droshar/Southern Principalities/Southern Principalities\|Southern Principalities]], the compound has housed generations of operatives, scouts, messengers, and assassins. While officially belonging to the [[Shigure Family\|Shigure Family]], its gates have quietly welcomed countless agents needing a place to recover, regroup, or disappear.

To outsiders it appears to be a secluded family estate.

To those who know its true purpose, it is something closer to a sanctuary.

Many members of the Choir spend years passing through its gates without ever learning its exact location.

---

## Description

The compound sits within a sheltered valley surrounded by towering pine forests and steep hills.

Earthen walls reinforced with timber surround the grounds, creating a protective perimeter that blends naturally into the landscape. Cherry trees, stone lanterns, and carefully maintained gardens soften the appearance of the defenses, making the estate feel more like a traditional family residence than a covert operational center.

A narrow stream enters through the northern wall and winds through the property before disappearing into the forest again.

The sound of flowing water is constant.

Even during silence, the compound never feels still.

## Layout

### Family Shrine

The spiritual center of the compound.

Dedicated to generations of the Shigure family.

Small offerings are maintained daily by [[Compendium/NPC's/Southern Principalities/Ayame Shigure\|Ayame Shigure]].

Many operatives quietly leave their own tokens before departing on missions.

---

### Main Residence

Home of the Shigure family.

Contains:
- communal dining hall
- kitchens
- guest rooms
- private quarters

Most visitors spend their first evening here sharing a meal.

---

### The Archive

A secure records hall maintained jointly by the Shigure family and trusted members of [[Compendium/Lore/Organizations/The Lotus Choir\|The Lotus Choir]].

Contains:
- historical records
- mission dossiers
- maps
- encoded correspondence
- operational intelligence

Mission packages are often delivered here.

---

### Training Grounds

An open courtyard and practice yard used for:

- weapon drills
- sparring
- physical conditioning

Many young operatives receive their first lessons here.

---

### Bathhouse

Fed by natural geothermal springs.

A favorite gathering place among residents.

Conversations held within the baths are traditionally considered private.

Whether that custom is actually respected is another matter entirely.

---

### Guest Houses

Several detached residences intended for visiting operatives and their families.

The compound can comfortably house:

- permanent residents
- visiting agents
- temporary refugees
- long-term guests

without appearing overcrowded.

---

### Gardens

Medicinal herbs, vegetables, fruit trees, and decorative plantings surround much of the compound.

Many residents spend evenings here.

Even hardened operatives tend to relax among the gardens.

---

## Security

Despite its peaceful appearance, the compound is exceptionally difficult to approach unnoticed.

Security measures include:

- hidden observation posts
- concealed paths
- layered warning systems
- trained scouts
- coded markers known only to trusted agents

Most intruders never realize they have been detected.

---

## Reputation

Among members of [[Compendium/Lore/Organizations/The Lotus Choir\|The Lotus Choir]]:

> "If the Compound can't keep you safe, nowhere can."

Among veteran operatives:

> "Home."

Among enemies of the Choir:

The location is little more than rumor.
