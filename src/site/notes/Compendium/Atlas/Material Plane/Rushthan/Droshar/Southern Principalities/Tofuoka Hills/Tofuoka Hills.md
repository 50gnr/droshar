# Tofuoka Hills

> "The hills smell of sulphur, rain, and coin."

## Overview

The Tofuoka Hills are a broad region of rolling highlands situated along the western frontier of the [[Southern Principalities \|Southern Principalities ]], where the mountains gradually descend toward the great desert kingdoms beyond.

Rich in sulphur, mineral deposits, and geothermal activity, the hills have supported thriving settlements for centuries despite their harsh terrain.

The region is best known for the city of [[Compendium/Atlas/Material Plane/Rushthan/Droshar/Southern Principalities/Tofuoka Hills/Kihōsō\|Kihōsō]], extensive mining operations, and the recent appearance of the Great Kaiju [[Compendium/Lore/Deities/Kaiju/Nomi\|Nomi]].

---

## Geography

The Tofuoka Hills form a rugged transition between mountain and desert.

Features include:

- Rolling rocky hills
- Sulphur vents
- Hot springs
- Mineral-rich streams
- Deep underground aquifers
- Wind-carved valleys

Though rainfall is limited, underground water has historically sustained both settlements and agriculture.

---

## Kihōsō

The largest settlement in the region is [[Compendium/Atlas/Material Plane/Rushthan/Droshar/Southern Principalities/Tofuoka Hills/Kihōsō\|Kihōsō]], a prosperous mining city built around access to sulphur deposits and deep wells.

Kihōsō serves as the region's primary trade hub and gateway between the Southern Principalities and western lands.

---

## Nomi

The Great Kaiju [[Compendium/Lore/Deities/Kaiju/Nomi\|Nomi]] currently inhabits the region.

Its movements between sulphur deposits and underground water reserves have transformed much of the surrounding landscape.

The creature's activities have increasingly threatened the water supply upon which local communities depend.

---

## Economy

Major exports include:

- Sulphur
- Rare ores
- Glass
- Alchemical reagents
- Domesticated giant beetles

Trade caravans frequently pass through the region on their way to distant markets.

---

## Reputation

People elsewhere in the South often describe the Tofuoka Hills as:

- Wealthy
- Harsh
- Independent
- Resourceful

A common saying states:

> "Nothing grows in Tofuoka without stubbornness."
