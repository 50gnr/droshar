# Kihōsō

> "If it burns, bubbles, explodes, or makes money, you'll find it in Kihōsō."

## Overview

Kihōsō is the largest settlement in the [[Compendium/Atlas/Material Plane/Rushthan/Droshar/Southern Principalities/Tofuoka Hills/Tofuoka Hills\|Tofuoka Hills]] and serves as the primary trade hub between the western frontier and the interior of the [[Southern Principalities \|Southern Principalities ]].

Built amongst rolling mineral-rich hills and geothermal springs, the city has prospered through mining, sulphur extraction, glassmaking, and caravan trade.

The city is known for its resilience, wealth, and distinctive smell.

Visitors often describe Kihōsō before they ever see it.

---

## First Impressions

Long before reaching the city walls, travelers notice:

- sulphur carried on the wind
- columns of steam rising from the hills
- beetle caravans crossing distant roads
- terraces carved into rocky slopes
- hundreds of lanterns glowing after sunset

Despite its rugged appearance, Kihōsō is one of the wealthiest cities in the western South.

---

## Architecture

Kihōsō was built to endure difficult terrain rather than impress visitors.

Common features include:

- terraced stone construction
- elevated walkways
- geothermal bathhouses
- reinforced retaining walls
- covered markets
- windbreak towers

Buildings tend to be practical and durable.

Beauty is usually found in craftsmanship rather than ornamentation.

---

## The Deep Wells

The city's greatest treasure lies beneath it.

Enormous wells reach deep into ancient aquifers hidden beneath layers of stone.

These waters have sustained the city for generations.

Control of the wells is considered a matter of public survival.

Recent concerns regarding water quality have become a major source of tension throughout the region.

---

## Industry

Kihōsō's economy revolves around:

- sulphur mining
- rare ores
- glass production
- alchemical materials
- caravan trade
- giant beetle husbandry

Goods from Kihōsō can be found throughout the Southern Principalities.

---

## Beetle Caravans

Large domesticated beetles are commonly used throughout the region.

These creatures haul freight, pull wagons, and transport supplies through terrain unsuitable for traditional draft animals.

Caravans arriving from Kihōsō are immediately recognizable by the distinctive clicking and rattling sounds of their beetle teams.

---

## Current Concerns

In recent months, increasing attention has focused on the movements of [[Compendium/Lore/Deities/Kaiju/Nomi\|Nomi]].

The Great Kaiju's activities have begun affecting local water supplies and mining operations.

Though city officials publicly remain optimistic, many residents fear conditions may worsen.

---

## Culture

Residents of Kihōsō value:

- hard work
- practicality
- craftsmanship
- self-reliance

Outsiders are welcomed readily enough, provided they contribute something useful.

A common local saying states:

> "A problem is only a problem until somebody builds a tool for it."
