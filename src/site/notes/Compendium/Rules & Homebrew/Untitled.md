>  **Command the Room**
> 
> As an **action**, Maki commands the attention of creatures around him. Choose a number of creatures up to Maki’s **proficiency bonus** within 30 feet that can hear and understand him.
> 
> Each target must make a **Wisdom saving** throw against Maki’s spell save DC. On a failed save, the creature falls under Maki’s influence for **10 minutes**.
> 
> While under this influence:
> 
> - Maki has **advantage** on **Charisma checks** made against the creature.
> - The creature cannot take **reactions**.
> - The creature has **disadvantage** on **attack rolls** against creatures other than Maki.
> - The creature cannot knowingly interrupt Maki while he is speaking or performing, though it can respond once he finishes his immediate statement.
> 
> The effect ends early for a creature if Maki damages it, Maki becomes incapacitated, or the creature can no longer hear Maki.
> 
> On a successful save, a creature is immune to **Command the Room** for 24 hours.
> 
> Once Maki uses this feature, he cannot use it again until he finishes a **short or long rest**.
