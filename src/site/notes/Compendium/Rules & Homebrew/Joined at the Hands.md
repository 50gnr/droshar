**Joined at the Hands  **
  
As a **bonus action**, if Sho and Maeva are within 5 feet of each other, they may press their prosthetic hands together. The two prosthetics seamlessly fuse into a single living construct, linking them for up to *1 minute*. The link ends early if either creature is **incapacitated**, or either creature ends the effect as a **bonus action**.

While linked, the following rules apply:

- **Shared Burden.** Both creatures move at **half speed** and have disadvantage on **Dexterity saving throws**.
    
- **Shared Focus.** If either creature casts a spell that requires concentration, both creatures are considered to be concentrating on that spell. *Essentially providing Advantage on Concentration. (One roll each)*
    
- **Shared Touch.** When either creature casts a spell with a range of **Touch** that targets only themselves or the other linked creature, both linked creatures become valid targets and each gains the spell's effects simultaneously, provided both are eligible targets.
    
When the link ends for any reason, both linked become **Incapacitated** and have a **Speed of 0** until the end of their next turn, as a wave of lethargy washes over both of you.
