# Magic in Droshar

Magic exists throughout the world of Droshar.

No single culture agrees on exactly what magic is, where it originated, or why it functions.

What is known is that magic remains even though the gods do not.

---

## The Three Sources of Power

Most supernatural phenomena in Droshar fall into one of three categories:

### Magic

The manipulation of ambient magical energy through training, talent, instinct, study, belief, or force of will.

This includes:

- Wizards
- Sorcerers
- Bards
- Warlocks
- Clerics
- Paladins
- Druids
- Rangers

Though their methods differ, they all produce magical effects through interaction with the world's existing magical forces.

---

### Biomancy

Biomancy is the direct manipulation of ley energy and living essence.

Unlike traditional magic, biomancy changes living things.

Biomancy is responsible for:

- Mutation
- Adaptation
- Many monsters
- Ley-touched creatures
- Titan corruption
- Biological alteration

Some view biomancy as evolution accelerated.

Others view it as corruption.

Most agree it is dangerous.

---

### Magitech

Magitech combines engineering with controlled magical power.

Most magitech devices rely on **essence crystals**, which store and regulate magical energy.

Examples include:

- Arcanomagnetic engines
- Imperial machinery
- Crystal-powered tools
- Advanced transportation
- Military technology

Magitech is generally more stable and predictable than biomancy, though it requires resources, maintenance, and specialized knowledge.

---

## Divine Magic

The gods are gone.

No known deity actively grants power.

Clerics and paladins still exist.

Their abilities are believed to arise from:

- Faith
- Conviction
- Oaths
- Belief
- Spiritual discipline

Whether this power comes from the individual soul, the world itself, or some lingering remnant of older forces remains a matter of debate.

No one has received a confirmed divine answer in living memory.

---

## Leylines

Leylines are currents of magical energy that run throughout the world.

Many ancient sites, ruins, and natural wonders form around concentrations of ley energy.

Areas where leylines converge often experience:

- Strange phenomena
- Magical instability
- Biomantic mutation
- Enhanced spellcasting
- Unpredictable effects

Many scholars believe leylines existed long before modern civilizations.

---

## Essence Crystals

Essence crystals are naturally occurring materials capable of storing magical energy.

They are one of the foundations of modern magitech.

Essence crystals can be used to:

- Power devices
- Fuel machinery
- Store magical energy
- Stabilize magical processes

The Empire's technological advancement is heavily dependent on access to these crystals.

---

## The Titans

The relationship between magic, leylines, and the Titans remains poorly understood.

Many scholars believe Titan activity altered the world permanently.

Others argue the Titans merely interacted with forces that already existed.

The truth remains uncertain.

---

## What Most People Believe

Most ordinary people do not separate magic into academic categories.

To them there are simply:

- Spellcasters
- Biomancers
- Magitech engineers

And everyone agrees on one thing:

Magic is useful.

Biomancy is dangerous.

And nobody truly understands either.
