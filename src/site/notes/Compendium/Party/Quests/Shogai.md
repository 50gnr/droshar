# Quest Info

## Summary

Reports continue to surface throughout the wilderness of the [[Compendium/Atlas/Material Plane/Rushthan/Droshar/Southern Principalities/Southern Principalities\|Southern Principalities]] regarding entities known as **Shogai**, more commonly called **Skinwalkers**.

Witnesses claim these creatures imitate people, animals, and familiar voices in order to lure victims away from safety.

Most stories originate from remote forests, mountain paths, and isolated shrines.

Whether the Shogai are real creatures, wandering spirits, or merely a collection of old superstitions remains unknown.

# Investigation

## Common Stories

The Shogai are said to appear as whatever their victim is most likely to trust.

Reported forms include:

- Lost children
- Injured animals
- Wandering travelers
- Missing family members
- Crying voices in the dark
- Familiar faces that should not be present

Most tales claim they seek to lower a person's guard before revealing their true nature.

---

## The Eyes

Nearly every story agrees on one detail.

The eyes.

No matter what form a Shogai assumes, something about its eyes remains wrong.

Descriptions include:

- Eyes that never blink
- Reflections that move independently
- Pupils that appear mismatched
- Darkness where eyes should be

A common saying warns:

> "If you're close enough to check the eyes, you're already too close."

---

## Habitat

Shogai are traditionally associated with:

- Dense forests
- Isolated valleys
- Mountain trails
- Abandoned shrines
- Forgotten roads

They are rarely reported near towns or large groups of people.

Historically they were believed to appear only at night.

Recent rumors suggest some may now be active during daylight hours.

---

## Local Warnings

Children throughout the South are taught:

- Never follow a voice into the woods.
- Never leave the trail after sunset.
- Never trust someone who appears where they should not be.
- If an animal speaks, run.
- If a stranger knows your name, keep walking.
